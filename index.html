Hello!

I'm Fernando González, a comitted video game programmer, designer, and director with over ten years of experience making games.

Who I am

Originally from San Salvador, El Salvador, I’m a freelance game and software developer currently based in Barcelona, Spain. Having worked with a variety of frameworks and game engines for over a decade, designing and developing games is truly my passion and life’s work. I have worked with a broad range of clients and produced a wide variety of video games.

Having spent most of my young adulthood developing games with Unity 3D, I can swiftly design and implement a wide variety of systems in an effective and efficient manner. Although I’ve taken an active part in a wide variety of development fronts, from implementation of video game AI to coding graphics and compute shaders with GLSL, level design and gameplay programming are without a doubt my favorite part of the job. I particularly enjoy working with new tools and frameworks that force me to adapt and keep growing as a developer.

I am in my last year of studies as a software engineering major at ENTI, a faculty at Universitat de Barcelona. I am currently open to working for a studio or startup in need of a talented and highly experienced developer to help to produce their projects.
